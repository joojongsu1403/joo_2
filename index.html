Episode IV / Dystopia

With two feet, we stand in reality. With two fingers, we come in contact with the virtual world. Where do we exist in this world, at this moment in time? We struggle to exist in a society where the media endlessly inflates our ego and validates our sense of identity. At almost every possible instance, we encounter ourselves without sanity, without face, and without self, ultimately leaving us in a state of disillusionment. In collaboration with Berlin-based performer MM, GENTLE MONSTER presents, “DYSTOPIA”. Situated amongst cameras and screens, our main character finds himself confronted with multiple reflections of his self. Like all humans, he seeks validation from different forms of media and can never satisfy himself with just one.
